# Creating/Explaining the Order List

---

## Arduino Mega

**Purpose:** Main control system for the SRA.

The Arduino Mega was primarily chosen as the best candicate as it has the ability to controll 16 servos. With the 10 motors that I already have, and the additional sensors I will need, this microcontroller proides the best use-case for my project.

---

## USB Host Shield

**Purpose:** To connect external controllers to the Arduino Mega.

The USB host sheild will allow me to plug in a controller to controll my 10 servos, With 2 joysticks to independantly controll the servos, We also have buttons that can act as macros.

---

## Gyroscopic Tilt Sensor

**Purpose:** To measure the tilt and orientation of the SRA system.

When the SRA system is not getting commands from the controller or inactive, The gyro will allow the SRA to stabalize which allows for ease of controll, and a integrated camera stick.

---

## PLA Filament (4 Pack)

**Purpose:** Rapid Prototyping & Ease of Printing

PLA will be used to test structure and connections, as it has low warpage, ease of printing, and lastly Is less brittle than ABS which will allow me to make parts that bend to absorb impact.

---

## 3x100x100mm Aluminium Sheet

**Purpose:** The aluminium sheet will be used to fabricate the gear housing system

The aluminium sheets will be used to route the gear housing system as they are 3mm tall, and i can customize the lenth and width on my mill.

---

## Vise

**Purpose:** Creating 3D parts with custom fixtures

Currently I only have clamps that are able to hold parts that require 2D milling, But there are a few parts that require custom fixtures and 3d milling.

---

## ABS Filament (4 Pack)

**Purpose:** Lightweight structural parts.

ABS will be used during the final stages of prototyping, as well as the final design in order to maximize ridgidity while keeping the overall build lightweight.

---

## 14mm Aluminium Rod

**Purpose:** Raw material for gears.

The 14mm aluminum rods will be used to manufacture the gears used in the gearbox system. I will cut the rods to size ten mill them om my 4th axis
---

## Buckles and Straps Set

**Purpose:** Backpack mounting system.

The robotic arms will be mounted like a backpack, so straps are needed, I will add TPU pads later to ensure comfort.

---

## Aluminium 3/8" Plates

**Purpose:** Ring gear and structural gearbox components.

The thicker aluminum plates will be used to create ring gears, These ring gears need to be ridgid and withstand the load of the weight of the arm aswell as the object we are carrying.
